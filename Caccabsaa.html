Caccabsaa

Oromo's Fiery Spiced Meat

About Caccabsaa

Caccabsaa is a bold, spicy meat dish made with beef or lamb, slow-cooked with berbere spice, garlic, and onions. It’s a centerpiece at Oromo gatherings, served with injera or bread.

Slow-Cooked

Caccabsaa Recipe

Ingredients

  • Beef/lamb (cubed)
  • Berbere spice
  • Onions & garlic
  • Niter Kibbeh (spiced butter)

Cooking Steps

1
Sear the Meat

Brown meat in spiced butter with onions and garlic.

2
Add Spices

Stir in berbere and simmer with water for 1-2 hours.

3
Serve Hot

Garnish with fresh herbs and serve with injera.

A Dish of Unity

Caccabsaa is traditionally prepared by men during communal work events (Daboo), symbolizing teamwork and shared labor in Oromo culture.

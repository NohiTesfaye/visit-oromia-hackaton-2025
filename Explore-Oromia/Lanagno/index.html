Welcome to Lake Langano

Nestled in the heart of Ethiopia's Oromia region, Lake Langano is a stunning freshwater lake renowned for its unique brownish hue, safe swimming waters, and breathtaking natural beauty. As one of the few bilharzia-free lakes in Ethiopia, it offers visitors a rare opportunity to enjoy water activities without health concerns.

The lake spans approximately 18 kilometers in length and 16 kilometers in width, with its warm waters creating a perfect environment for relaxation and recreation. Surrounded by acacia forests and rich birdlife, Lake Langano is a sanctuary for nature lovers and adventure seekers alike.

Lake Langano Highlights

Safe Swimming

One of Ethiopia's few bilharzia-free lakes, perfect for swimming and water activities year-round.

Rich Birdlife

Home to over 300 bird species including fish eagles, kingfishers, and various waterfowl.

Beautiful Resorts

Numerous lakeside resorts offering comfortable accommodations with stunning lake views.

Best Time to Visit

Lake Langano welcomes visitors year-round, but certain seasons offer particularly ideal conditions.

Dry Season (October - May)

The most popular time to visit, with sunny days, minimal rainfall, and perfect conditions for swimming and outdoor activities. Temperatures range from 20°C to 30°C.

Green Season (June - September)

The landscape turns lush green during these months, with occasional short rains. Fewer crowds and lower prices make this an attractive time for budget travelers.

Activities at Lake Langano

Swimming

Enjoy safe swimming in the lake's warm, bilharzia-free waters perfect for all ages.

Birdwatching

Spot over 300 bird species including fish eagles, kingfishers, and herons.

Boating

Rent a boat or kayak to explore the lake's tranquil waters and hidden coves.

Hiking

Explore scenic trails around the lake with stunning viewpoints and wildlife.

How to Get There
By Road

Lake Langano is about 200km south of Addis Ababa via the Addis Ababa-Shashamane road. The drive takes approximately 3-4 hours.

Public Transport

Regular buses run from Addis Ababa to Shashamane. From there, you can take a taxi to Lake Langano (about 30 minutes).

By Air

The nearest airport is in Awassa (about 1.5 hours away). Domestic flights are available from Addis Ababa to Awassa.

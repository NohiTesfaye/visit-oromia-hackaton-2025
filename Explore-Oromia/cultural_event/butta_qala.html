Butta Qalaa

Oromo Warrior's Art of Stick Fighting

More Than a Sport

Butta Qalaa is a traditional Oromo martial art where young men duel with sticks (qalaa), testing courage and skill while avoiding serious injury. It’s a rite of passage for Qondaala (warrior class).

The Code of Butta

Respect

Never strike a fallen opponent or target the head.

Ceremony

Fights begin and end with war songs (geerarsa).

Conflict Resolution

Settles disputes between clans without bloodshed.

Witness Butta Qalaa

Annual tournaments are held during Gada inaugurations and harvest festivals. Winners earn prestige and bridal eligibility.
